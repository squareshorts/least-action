## Warnings And Caveats

- No independent secondary semantic predictor was present in the repository, so no secondary predictor values were invented.
- The existing mixed-effects output was generated by a `statsmodels` MixedLM with an item variance component and subject grouping, but the fitted specification did not estimate a separate subject random-intercept variance. The detailed mixed-effects table reports this explicitly.
- Very small mixed-effects p values underflowed numerically in `statsmodels`; these are reported as less than 1e-300 rather than as exact zeros.
- The supplement compiles, but several wide detail tables produce overfull-box layout warnings. The numerical content is intact; final journal formatting may benefit from landscape or resized table formatting.
